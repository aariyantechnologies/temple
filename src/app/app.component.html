About Us

Our journey dates back to the glorious and culturally rich Vijayanagara-Nayak period, somewhere between 1500 AD - 1600 AD, as indicated by the Archaeological Survey Department report. It was during this time that a valiant fight unfolded in Ayodhya, coinciding with the period when Babar demolished the Ram Mandir and built a mosque in the same place.
